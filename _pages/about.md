Hey everyone! My name is Aman, a Year 2 **Electrical Engineering** student at **The Hong Kong Polytechnic University**, with a secondary major in **AIDA (Artificial Intelligence and Data Analytics)**. Originally from Sri Lanka, I now study in Hong Kong.

I'm passionate about several fields within the endless realm of Electrical Engineering, including power systems, electronics, IoT, and robotics, and I'm always looking to explore new opportunities to diversify my knowledge and build meaningful skills.

As part of the **Undergraduate Research and Innovation Scheme (URIS)** at PolyU, I'm currently working on making power grids smarter and more resilient using AI-driven models for real-time state estimation.

I'm also heavily involved in teaching and tutoring. With over four years of experience, I've taught more than 40 students across international schools in Hong Kong. You can find out more about my services [here](#).

On weekends, you'll find me on the cricket pitch. I've played my whole life, and currently represent the **PolyU Cricket Team** and the **Kowloon Cricket Club (KCC)**.
