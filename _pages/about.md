Hey everyone! My name is Aman, a **Year 2 Electrical Engineering** student at **The Hong Kong Polytechnic University**, with a secondary major in **AIDA (Artificial Intelligence and Data Analytics)**. Originally from Colombo, Sri Lanka, I now study in Hong Kong.

I'm passionate about several fields within the endless realm of Electrical Engineering, including **power systems**, **circuits**, **electronics**, **IoT**, and **robotics**, and I'm always looking to explore new opportunities to diversify and expand my knowledge while gaining important skills.

As part of the **CURI (College of Undergraduate Research and Innovation)** at PolyU, I'm currently working on making power grids smarter and more resilient under the URIS. My project uses **AI-driven models** for real-time state estimation, helping modern grids better predict and respond to disturbances.

Beyond research, I'm an avid tutor and mentor. It's a big part of who I am and who I want to be. I've been teaching international students since I was 15, and over the past few years I've expanded to teaching students from all over the world online. In just over a year in Hong Kong, I've already taught more than 40 students from many reputed international schools across a diverse range of curricula (IGCSE, A-Level, IB). I genuinely love mentoring and helping students build confidence in their learning—it's become both a passion and a rewarding part of my week. You can find out more [here](#).

On weekends, you'll find me on the cricket pitch. I've played cricket my whole life, and currently represent the **PolyU Cricket Team** and **Kowloon Cricket Club (KCC)**.
