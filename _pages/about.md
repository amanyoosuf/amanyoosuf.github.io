I'm a Year 2 Electrical Engineering student at **The Hong Kong Polytechnic University**, with a secondary major in **AIDA (Artificial Intelligence and Data Analytics)**. My research focuses on **Dynamic State Estimation (DSE)** of modern power grids, where I apply AI and deep learning to enhance grid reliability.

I'm passionate about **power systems**, **circuits**, **electronics**, **IoT**, and **robotics**—and I'm always eager to explore new opportunities across electrical engineering.

---

## 🧪 Research

As part of the **URIS (Undergraduate Research and Innovation Scheme)** at PolyU, I'm working on making power grids smarter and more resilient. My current project uses **AI-driven models** for real-time state estimation, helping utilities better predict and respond to grid disturbances.

---

## 💡 Beyond the Lab

I've been teaching international students in Hong Kong since I was 15, covering a wide range of curricula (IGCSE, A-Level, IB). I genuinely love mentoring and helping students build confidence in their learning—it's become both a passion and a rewarding part of my week.

On weekends, you'll find me on the cricket pitch. I've played cricket my whole life, and currently represent the **PolyU Cricket Team** and **Kowloon Cricket Club (KCC)**.

I also love **traveling**—studying abroad in Hong Kong has opened my eyes to new cultures, perspectives, and opportunities. I'm actively working towards programs like the **Cambridge Girton Programme**, **exchange semesters**, and **MITACS**.
